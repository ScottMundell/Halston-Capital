Scott M. Mundell is the CEO and Managing Director of Halston Capital, based in Perth, Western Australia. Mr. Mundell is a Canadian citizen and resides in Perth, Australia and spends several months of the year in Western Canada.

Mr. Mundell attended Simon Fraser University and the British Columbia Institute of Technology before completing his studies and receiving his Bachelor of Commerce degree. Mr. Mundell also earned various accreditation's through the Canadian Securities Institute, (CSC, CMF, LLQP), to become qualified as a licensed investment advisor.

After several years and tenures with various investment advisory and banking organisations, Mr. Mundell began his own practice in corporate advisory and corporate finance.

Halston Capital is an Australian based corporate advisory and investment banking facilitator with a focus towards emerging markets around the globe. In conjunction with our team of associated commercial and technical consultants, our primary focus is towards infrastructure in emerging growth economies and project financing for global infrastructure projects.

In addition to our emerging growth strategies, Halston Capital also act as corporate advisers to several family offices in Asia and UAE, in regards to their humanitarian and infrastructure funding of various projects and humanitarian interests.
